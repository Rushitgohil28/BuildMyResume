🔒 Privacy & Storage

Your resume data, profile photo, and download history are securely stored locally in your browser. We do not use external servers or databases to store your personal information. This means your data remains entirely private and under your control.

💾 Data Backup & Restore

Because your data is saved in your browser, it will be lost if you clear your browsing history or switch to a different device. Use these tools to download a backup of your data or restore it from a previous backup.

⚠️ Delete Resume Data

This action will permanently delete your saved resume details, profile photo, and PDF download history from this browser. This action cannot be undone unless you have downloaded a backup file.
